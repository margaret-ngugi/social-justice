Our short story

It was a warm summer night when I first had the idea for my

social justice web application. I had just finished a passionate

conversation with some friends about the need for accessible resources

for those marginalized by society. We discussed the various

issues that people face, including police brutality,

income inequality, and racial and gender discrimination.

The idea struck me like a bolt of lightning. Why not create

a centralized platform where anyone can come together to

fight for social justice? And so, my journey began.

I spent countless hours researching and learning about the various

issues plaguing our society. I studied the laws and regulations

that protect the rights of individuals, and I spoke

with people directly affected by these injustices.

our vision

My aim was simple: to create a web application that would allow

anyone with a passion for social justice to come together and

make a difference. I wanted to provide a platform for marginalized

communities to share their experiences, and for allies to show their

support

This platform aims to empower individuals to fight against

discrimination and promote social justice. Stay informed

about incidents happening around the resources and tools to

track discrimination and report it.
